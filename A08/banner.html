Characteristics

Shizuka Minamoto – A nice and smart girl. She likes studying, playing the violin, and taking baths. She is Nobita’s crush and often joins their adventures.

Suneo Honekawa – A rich boy who likes to show off his toys. Sometimes he teases Nobita with Gian, but he can be caring too.

Doraemon – A robot cat from the future sent to help Nobita. He has many gadgets in his pocket that bring both help and trouble. He is wise and a good friend.

Takeshi Gouda (Gian) – The biggest and strongest in the group. He has a bad temper and sings badly. He can be mean but cares about his friends when it matters.

Nobita Nobi – The main character. He is lazy, clumsy, and unlucky but kind-hearted. Doraemon helps him with his problems and teaches him lessons during their adventures.

Doraemon's Gadgets

Take-copter – A small propeller that allows people to fly.

Anywhere Door – A door that lets you go anywhere instantly.

Time Machine – Allows time travel to past or future.

Fun Facts

• Doraemon was created by Fujiko F. Fujio in 1969.

• The blue color of Doraemon is because he lost his ears due to a mouse bite!

• The series has inspired movies, games, and merchandise worldwide.

• Doraemon is a cultural icon in Japan and many Asian countries.

Famous Quotes

Doraemon: "Don't give up! If you fail, try again!"

Nobita: "True friendship is a treasure."
